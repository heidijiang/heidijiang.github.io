HEIDI JIANG

Hi! I'm Heidi. I'm from the Bay Area, but am currently finishing up a Neuroscience Ph.D. in Chicago. I study human olfactory (or smell) processing using electrocorticography and other functional imaging methods. In particular I enjoy learning about and implementing various ML methods for decoding/predicting odor perception.

Outside of work my favorite hobby is watching my plants die slowly. If you want to challenge me at Twilight Struggle, I insist on playing as the USSR. Twice in my life, I have performed in a double bass sextet (where all six members of the group play the double bass). It did not sound good.
