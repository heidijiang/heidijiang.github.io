PILAF'S FAN PAGE

This is a place to learn about Pilaf the dog. Serious fans only, please.

Pilaf is a six year old Havanese dog, born in Tracy, CA. Desperate to escape the nightmarish hellscape of the Sacramento Valley (just like the titular character from the recent Oscar-nominated film, Lady Bird), he was eventually rescued by Heidi when he was 6 months old. Not quite a boy, but not yet a man, he was crowned "Emperor Pilaf" -- a minor, often foiled villain from the children's TV show, Dragon Ball.

Pilaf currently lives on the north side of Chicago. He is 17 pounds (the breed range is 7-13 lbs), but his parents maintain he is "just big-boned." As a Cuban Havanese, he is anxiously following the progress of the DACA legislation.
