HEIDI JIANG

Hi! I'm Heidi. I'm from the Bay Area and just finished up a Neuroscience Ph.D at Northwestern University, where I studied human olfactory (or smell) processing using electrocorticography and other functional imaging methods. In particular some of my projects involved implementing various ML methods for decoding/predicting odor perception, and I'm excited about applying the skills I've picked up over my time in academia to data science applications in industry. This summer, I will be completing a data science fellowship at Insight in NYC.
